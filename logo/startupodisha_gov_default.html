Fund of Funds

To Foster Entrepreneurship & Promote Innovation, MSME Department has released a “Fund Of Funds” Scheme With Startup Odisha as the Implementing Agency. This Scheme Is Dedicated To Focus On The Capital Investment Requirement Of Startup’s During Their Seed Stage. Looking At The Essentiality Of Venture Capital & Angel Fund For Registered Startup, The Fund Of Funds With A Target Corpus of Rs 100 Crore will Be Disbursed Over 5 Years To Support 5,000 Startup’s In The State.
